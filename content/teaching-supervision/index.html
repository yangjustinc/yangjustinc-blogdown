University College London

I’m a teaching assistant for the Practical Statistics for Mental Health Research module on the MSc in Mental Health Sciences Research course. I also mark for the Current Research in Depression and Anxiety and Current Research in Psychosis and Bipolar Disorder modules. I’m always happy to co-supervise MSc students completing their dissertations using the Research Database at Camden & Islington NHS Foundation Trust.

Queen Mary University of London

I’m a dissertation supervisor on the MSc in Global Public Health and Policy course where I mainly supervise projects pertaining to secondary data analysis, substance-related use disorders, and comparative health policy. I normally supervise 1-2 students per term.

University of Cambridge

I conducted supervisions on quantitative research methods and secondary data analysis to students on the MPhil in Population Health Sciences course.

The University of British Columbia

During the final year of my undergraduate studies, I designed and coordinated a student directed seminar on modernist literature and obscenity law.
