Hello! Welcome to my new personal academic landing page. Having previously tried Hugo Academic and al-folio, both of which were very nice, I found that updating themes was annoying and bothersome sometimes, often requiring me to sit down and rebase/merge differences across code where I modified themes for my taste and purpose.

So now it’s back to basics with Yihui Xie’s marvelous great XMin theme where I can focus entirely on content and not have to worry too much about theming and visuals and such. It’s not necessarily “pretty” but I think there is also an elegance to simplicity.

I’m hoping to use this site more robustly to share my thoughts, random things I come across in my work, and maybe other things if they are of interest.
