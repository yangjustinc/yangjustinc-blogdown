Teaching & Supervision

I’m a Teaching Assistant for the Practical Statistics for Mental Health Research module on the UCL MSc in Mental Health Sciences Research course. I also mark for the Current Research in Depression and Anxiety and Current Research in Psychosis and Bipolar Disorder modules. I’m always happy to co-supervise MSc students completing their dissertations using the Research Database at Camden & Islington NHS Foundation Trust.

I’m a dissertation supervisor on the QMUL MSc in Global Public Health and Policy course where I mainly supervise projects pertaining to secondary data analysis, substance-related use disorders, and comparative health policy. I normally supervise 1-2 students per term.

At Cambridge, I conducted supervisions on quantitative research methods to students on the MPhil in Population Health Sciences course.
